PulseForge runtime custom-song import requires ffmpeg.exe in this folder.

From the repository root, run:
powershell -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File .\tools\runtime_audio\setup_ffmpeg.ps1

The executable is intentionally not committed to Git. Before distributing a
tester build, review the license of the exact FFmpeg binary you ship and include
the notices/source-access materials required by that build's license.

FFmpeg project: https://ffmpeg.org/
FFmpeg legal information: https://ffmpeg.org/legal.html
